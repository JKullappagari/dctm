Workspace View

 

The DCTrackMobileWeb workspace view consists of the following details that are always visible on whatever functional page you are viewing or working on.

1.      Topmost of the screen, you will see the DCTrackMobileWeb Solution.

 

 

2.      On the next layer is the Navigation Menu bar.

 

 

3.      At the top right corner of the screen, you will see Welcome followed by the Username (ID) of the user currently logged into DCTrackMobileWeb with current date.

 

4.      At the bottom center of the screen, you will see the Copyright information.
