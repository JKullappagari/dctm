Maintain Purpose

 

The Purpose screen is used to define Check-Out purpose, which will be specified during check-out and Check-in using DCTrackMobile application. By defining a purpose, one can group the asset movements belonging to that purpose. A typical usage of purpose is as follows (to be modeled depending on your business needs):

 

Table 2: Example of Purpose

Purpose Name

Usage

Relocation – between Data Centers. One can define multiple move groups for tracking purposes.

To group all the movements for the relocation.

General Move – within a DC

To track the movements for general movement of assets within a DC; i.e. from one rack to another.

Return to Vendor

Sometimes equipment is taken as a ‘Loan’ from the vendor. Once can create a purpose to group the movement when the equipment is returned to the vendor.

Decommissioning

To group the movements when decommissioned assets are moved.

Disposal

To group the movements when assets are moved for disposal.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Can I create a Purpose with the same name?

No. Purpose should be unique. System will inform you that the record already exists.
