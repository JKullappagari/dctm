Company and Division Assignment

 

The Company – Division Assignment screen will enable privileged user to assign and maintain the various Divisions assignment to the Company.  Division is a sub group or department within the company, to which applications running in the company will be assigned. For example, applications which are running HR related processes will be part of HR division. Divisions are created under Application Menu.
