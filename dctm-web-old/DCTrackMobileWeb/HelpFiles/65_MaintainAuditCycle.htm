Maintain Audit Cycle

 

The Audit Cycle screen will enable privileged users to define and maintain audit cycle information.

What is Audit Cycle?

Audit cycle is a period during which physical audit will be performed for a location. In DCTrackMobile the audit location is a ‘Room’.  During audit period check-out and Check-In will not be allowed in that room. Typically, a physical audit is done to reconcile the assets and during that period no movement of the assets are allowed, or in other words the inventory is ‘frozen’. Audit Cycle functionality allows the user to do that.
